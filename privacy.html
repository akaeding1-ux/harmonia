HARMONIA

Privacy Policy · Last updated May 25, 2026

Short version: Harmonia collects no personal data. Nothing you do in the app is transmitted to any server, stored remotely, or shared with anyone — ever.

What we collect

Nothing. Harmonia is a local audio and visual toy. It runs entirely in your browser or on your device. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no advertising.

Data stored on your device

Harmonia uses your browser's localStorage solely to save your compositions in the optional Save Slots feature. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser or app storage.

Permissions

Harmonia may request access to:

· Microphone — only if you use the recording feature, and only while recording. Audio is processed locally and is not uploaded anywhere.

· Haptics / Vibration — to give tactile feedback when you draw lines and trigger notes. This is optional and uses only the standard device vibration API.

Third parties

None. Harmonia does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or data brokers.

Children

Harmonia is suitable for all ages. Because it collects no data whatsoever, there are no special provisions required for children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR).

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Because we collect no data, any future changes are unlikely to affect you.
